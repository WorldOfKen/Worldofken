Chester Bennington

singer, songwriter, and occasional actor


Chester Bennington, Lead singer of Linkin Park on stage

A Timeline of Chester's music life

  • 1993:
    Bennington first began singing with a band called Sean Dowdell and His Friends? and together they released an eponymous three-track cassette
  • 1994:
    Joined Grey Daze, recording a demo in 1993 and two albums: Wake Me in 1994, and ...No Sun Today in 1997.
  • 1998:
    Bennington left Grey Daze
  • 1998-2000:
    Bennington Auditioned and started recording with Linkin Park (then 'Xero')
  • 2005:
    Bennington co-founded Dead by Sunrise
  • 2013-15:
    Stone Temple Pilots recruit Bennington to fill lead singer spot, Bennington leaves the band in 2015 due to commitments to Linkin Park
  • 2017:
    Bennington was found dead by his housekeeper at his home, His death was ruled a suicide by hanging leaving no suicide note.
