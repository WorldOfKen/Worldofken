What do warriors do?

The warrior in World of Warcraft can be used as either a damage dealer or a tank for your groups

  • Damage dealers take up the majority of the group compisition and main goal is to deal as much damage to the enemy while staying out of danger

  • Tanks are heavy defenders whos goal it is to keep the focus of the enemy while positioning them so as to maximize the potential of the damage dealers and minimise the amount of damage the rest of the group takes.
Arms

  • Arms is one of the two main damage dealing specilazations of the warrior

  • Arms warriors use a large two handed weapon to devastate their enemies

Weapon choices of Arms

  • two-handed swords
  • two-handed axes
  • two-handed maces
Protection

Protection is the Tank specilazation of warriors

  • Protection warriors tend to use a one-handed weapon and a sheild in their off-hand, though some are known to use two one-handed swords when the protection of a shield is unneeded

Weapon choices of protection

  • One-handed swords
  • one-handed axes
  • one-handed maces
  • shields in their off-hand
Fury

Fury is the second damage dealing specilazation of warriors

Fury warriors dual wield weapons and are akin to beserkers who are willing to take more hits to land more hits on the enemies.

Weapon choices of protection

  • One-handed swords
  • one-handed axes
  • one-handed maces
  • two-handed swords
  • two-handed axes
  • two-handed maces
Summary

Arms, Protection, Fury. These are the three choices of warriors and give you a varity of ways to be valuable to your group!
